\item{${}^{\rm a}\!\!$} {\eightpoint 
To ensure stationarity, we add a penalty term to the likelihood 
function proportional to $\max(|\lambda_{\scriptstyle {\rm max}}|-.995,0)^2$, 
where $\lambda_{\scriptstyle {\rm max}}$ is the maximal eigenvalue of $P$.
Numbers in parentheses are 90\% confidence
intervals for a bootstrapped distribution with 500 replications.
To ensure that the variance-covariance matrix $V$ is positive
semi-definite, we estimate $Q$ rather than $V=QQ'$.}
